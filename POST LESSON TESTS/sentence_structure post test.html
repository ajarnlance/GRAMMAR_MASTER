Sentence Structure Exam

Question 1 Incorrect

I wanted to go to the park, ______ it started to rain.

Your Answer:so
Correct Answer:but

Explanation:

This is a Compound Sentence using 'but' to show contrast. A comma is required before the conjunction.
Question 2 Incorrect

______ she studied very hard, she passed the exam with flying colors.

Your Answer:But
Correct Answer:Because

Explanation:

This is a Complex Sentence. 'Because' is a subordinating conjunction that starts the dependent clause.
Question 3 Correct

The students opened their books ______ started reading immediately.

Your Answer:and

Explanation:

This is a Simple Sentence with a compound verb (opened... and started). No comma is needed because there are not two independent clauses.
Question 4 Correct

Which sentence is punctuated CORRECTLY?

Your Answer:I like coffee, but my brother prefers tea.

Explanation:

In a Compound Sentence, place the comma BEFORE the coordinating conjunction (FANBOYS).
Question 5 Correct

We can go to the Italian restaurant, ______ we can try the new sushi place.

Your Answer:or

Explanation:

'Or' is the correct conjunction to show a choice between two options in a Compound Sentence.
Question 6 Incorrect

Which of the following is a COMPLEX sentence?

Your Answer:The dog barked, and the mailman ran away.
Correct Answer:Although the dog barked, the mailman was not afraid.

Explanation:

Option 3 has a dependent clause ('Although the dog barked') and an independent clause. The others are Simple or Compound.
Question 7 Correct

He was late to work ______ he forgot to set his alarm.

Your Answer:because

Explanation:

We use 'because' to show the reason/cause. Note: No comma is usually needed when the dependent clause comes second.
Question 8 Correct

The movie was three hours long, ______ it was incredibly interesting.

Your Answer:yet

Explanation:

'Yet' is used to show unexpected contrast (similar to 'but'). It connects two independent clauses.
Question 9 Correct

Which sentence requires a comma?

Your Answer:When I arrive home I will call you.

Explanation:

When a dependent clause ('When I arrive home') starts the sentence, it must be followed by a comma.
Question 10 Correct

Choose the SIMPLE sentence:

Your Answer:My friend plays soccer and basketball every weekend.

Explanation:

This is a Simple Sentence with a compound object (soccer and basketball). It has only one subject-verb relationship.
